Home Health Aide

Certified Nursing Assistant (CNA)

After surgery or being in a hospital bed for days, being a senior can decrease strength and make tasks that used to be straightforward become challenging.

As a result, activity daily living (ADL) assistance is required to promote self-care and independence.

Services

Service

Ambulation

Providing assistance to the patient in moving about inside or outside of their home in a safe way.

Service

Bathing

Assist the patient in entering and exiting the shower as well as the bathtub.

Service

Dressing

As the situation requires, we should assist the patient in donning and removing their clothing.

Service

Eating

Help patients with feeding if required.

Service

Personal Hygiene

Offer the patient help with shaving as well as the care of their oral, hair, skin, and nail health.

Service

Toileting

To prevent the patient's skin from breaking down, we should aid them in going to the bathroom, assisting them in disrobing and positioning themselves on the toilet, reminding them to use the toilet, and assisting them in changing their adult diapers.
