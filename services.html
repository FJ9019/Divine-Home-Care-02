Our Services

Ambulation

Providing assistance to the patient in moving about inside or outside of their home in a safe way..

Bathing

Assist the patient in entering and exiting the shower as well as the bathtub.

Dressing

As the situation requires, we should assist the patient in donning and removing their clothing.

Eating

Help patients with feeding if required.




Personal Hygiene

Offer the patient help with shaving as well as the care of their oral, hair, skin, and nail health.


Toileting

To prevent the patient's skin from breaking down, we should aid them in going to the bathroom.


Assisting them in disrobing and positioning themselves on the toilet, reminding them to use the toilet, and assisting them in changing their adult diapers.



Services

Divine Homecare provides many types of services, such as non-skilled and skilled by the most competent and professional Registered Nurse (RN), Licensed Practical Nurse (LPN), and Certified Nursing Assistant (CNA), including home assistance by a Home Health Aide, companionship, and homemaker.

At Device Home Care Services, all employees must pass a background screening, including fingerprints, either register or certify, and demonstrate competency in the area they apply for. Additionally, all employees are CPR certified. The Registered Nurse utilizes the four categories in nursing care: assessment, nursing diagnosis, intervention, and evaluation, to create the best appropriate care plan for each client. After the initial visit/admission, Odile, the Devine Home Care Services founder, will follow up, oversee and engage in your care plan until you reach an acceptable level of health and satisfaction with our services.
