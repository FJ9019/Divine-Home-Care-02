Companionship

Being a nurse during the pandemic has thought me the importance of companionship and friendship. Being lonely can affect someone mentally and physically, especially the elderly. Having someone around to engage in daily communication can make the day of an elderly person. It can be arranged based on the patient and loved one’s care needs.

A companion/homemaker can help with the following:

A) Cleaning and running errands.

B) Meal preparation.

C) Light housekeeping.

D) Transportation to a doctor's appointment.
