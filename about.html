About Us

Odile St Hubert

I am a registered nurse with over 15 years of experience. I have worked as a nurse educator in nursing homes, assisted living, home health care, rehabilitation, and hospitals.

During the pandemic, I also worked as a vaccination and travel nurse to help my fellow nurses and my community, including other states, combat COVID-19.

By working with a varied group of people and understanding each group's significant needs, you can rely on Divine Home Care to select the most competent, professional, dedicated, and compassionate individuals to help you achieve optimal health while living in the comfort of your home.

Our Mission

To connect and select the most competent, professional, and compassionate individuals to deliver the highest standard of care and to help you achieve optimal health while living in the comfort of your residence.

We aim to choose the most competent, dedicated individuals who can quickly develop a great rapport with their clients and families and understand and respect their patients' rights. Nonetheless, the most significant skill that Divine Home Care looks for in employees is the ones who have a compassionate and caring spirit toward their patients, which aids them in the healing process and helps them achieve their optimal health in the comfort of their residences.
